## Quick Summary

- 2-day hike in the <hl>Maderanertal</hl> at the <hl>Windgällenhütte AACZ</hl>.
- Excellent public transit access.
  - Took the bus from <hl>Erstfeld</hl> to <hl>Golzern, Talstation Seilbahn</hl>.
  - Returned via the same bus connection.
- There were many waterfalls along the length of the valley which, combined with
  the abundance of lush vegetation, made for some great views.

## Quick Summary

- Took the trail to the east of the <hl>Windgällenhütte</hl> up the valley.
- Made a small detour by <hl>Holenbalm</hl> towards <hl>Firnplanggen</hl>.
  - While there was indeed a thin trail going towards <hl>Firnplanggen</hl>, it
    was not officially marked.
- Returned to <hl>Golzern</hl> by taking the trail that ran parallel to the
  <hl>Chärstelenbach</hl>.
